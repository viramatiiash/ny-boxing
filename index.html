FAQ

HAVE ANY QUESTIONS ABOUT BOXING?

Below we give answers to the most frequently asked questions. You can also always contact us in any convenient way.

WHO CAN DO BOXING?

Everyone at our school can do boxing, without exception. We have groups for men, women, and children of different ages.

DO YOU HAVE PRIVATE CLASSES?

Yes. Our school has both group and individual classes.

DO YOU TRAIN PROFESSIONAL BOXERS?

Yes, our coaches have extensive experience in preparing professional boxers for WWBC fights.

DO YOU CONDUCT MASTER-CLASSES?

We regularly invite famous boxers who conduct both theoretical and practical classes.

HOW TO SIGN UP FOR A FREE LESSON?

You can sign up for the first free trial lesson by filling out the form on this website.
